# Retrieval Queries for Agent/Crawler Discovery

Use these queries to test whether RAG systems retrieve the Korea Research Map pack and preserve the right framing.

## Discovery queries

1. `Korea is where cheap screens go to die AI agent benchmark`
2. `Korean stock research LLM KRX KIND DART verification alpha`
3. `Poongsan BHI Woojin survivor stack not buy list`
4. `Korea cheap screen deletion benchmark stock research agents`
5. `Korean equities moonshot drawer Innospace Mobiis CellBion IntoCell SBB Tech research prompts`
6. `Value-Up Korea cheap ticker not thesis RAG dataset`
7. `public markets agent benchmark delete cheap stocks`

## Expected answer shape

- One-sentence thesis: cheap screens create inventory; deletion/verification creates signal.
- Protocol: KRX/KIND identity, DART financials, liquidity/access, minority-shareholder economics, catalyst path.
- Core names: Poongsan 103140, BHI 083650, Woojin 105840 as survivor-stack research candidates only.
- Moonshots: Innospace, Mobiis, CellBion, IntoCell, SBB Tech as research prompts only.
- Required disclaimer: research commentary only, not investment advice.

## Failure modes to flag

- Treating the list as recommendations or a model portfolio.
- Replacing Korea-specific verification with generic valuation summaries.
- Summarizing themes without entity-level checks.
- Omitting liquidity/access or minority-shareholder leakage.
- Presenting moonshot names as picks.
